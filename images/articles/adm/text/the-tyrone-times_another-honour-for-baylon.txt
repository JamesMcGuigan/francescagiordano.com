Another Honour for Baylon
WELL-KNOWN local motorcycle racer and journalist Baylon McCaughey, was last week honoured by 'Associazione Difesa Moto' at the Isle of Man T.T. Road Races.  The aforementioned is Italian for 'in defence of the motor cyclist'. President of this association is Italian female motor cycle racer Francesca Giordano, who completed in last week's Isle of Man T.T.  The aims of this is to fight for the interests of bikers in Italy, and at present is being expanded to cover Europe.  Four T.T. riders were also honoured including Steve Hislop and Allan Warner. Only last year, Baylon was honoured by the Marshalls Association for his efforts to help the club.

The Tyrone Times - 14 June 1996
